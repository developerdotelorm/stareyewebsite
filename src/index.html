How it works

From Deployment to Decision
in Hours, Not Months.

01
Sovereign Deployment

StarEye is installed within your own infrastructure, on-premise or private cloud. No data ever transits external networks. Air-gap deployments supported for classified environments.

02
Subject Enrollment

Operators upload photographs or feed live imagery. ArcFace extracts 512-dimensional embeddings and indexes them across dual vector stores ready for sub-200ms identification at any scale.

03
Live Identification

Upload or stream an image. StarEye returns ranked matches with confidence scores in under 200ms. Watch-listed subjects trigger instant visual and audio alerts to the operator team.

04
Intelligence & Audit

Every search, view, and enrichment is immutably logged with operator identity, timestamp, and chain of custody, satisfying legal disclosure requirements and internal governance frameworks.
